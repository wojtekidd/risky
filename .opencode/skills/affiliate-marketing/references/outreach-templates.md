# Outreach Templates

## 4-Email Cold Sequence

### Email 1: Initiation (Day 0)
```
Subject: [Name], your audience would find [product] valuable

Hi [Name],
Following your work on [topic]. [Product] solves [pain point] you mentioned.
Would appreciate 5 minutes to explore. Best, [Your Name]
```

### Email 2: Value Proposition (Day 3)
```
Subject: Quick follow-up on partnership

We offer: [X]% recurring, dedicated manager, [unique angle].
Open calendar: [link]. Best, [Your Name]
```

### Email 3: Social Proof (Day 7)
```
Subject: [Creator] just earned $X with us

[Similar creator] generated $[amount] first month. Limited spots.
Worth a 10-minute call? Best, [Your Name]
```

### Email 4: Final Touch (Day 14)
```
Subject: One more thing...
Last reach-out. [Commission], [cookie duration], dedicated support.
Join here: [affiliate signup link]. Best, [Your Name]
```
