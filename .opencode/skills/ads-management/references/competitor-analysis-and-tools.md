## Competitor Analysis Workflow

1. **Identify competitors**: search your keywords, check who's bidding
2. **Audit their ads**: Meta Ad Library + Google Transparency Center (free)
3. **Keyword gaps**: SpyFu or SEMrush — find keywords they rank for that you don't
4. **Creative analysis**: PowerAdSpy/BigSpy for cross-platform creative patterns
5. **Landing page review**: manually visit competitor landing pages; note CTAs, offers, layout
6. **Budget estimation**: SpyFu/SEMrush provide estimates (not exact, but directional)
7. **Document findings**: messaging patterns, offers, unique angles, gaps to exploit

## Google vs Meta — When to Use Which

| Scenario | Platform |
|----------|----------|
| High purchase intent, active search | Google Ads |
| Demand generation, brand awareness | Meta Ads |
| B2B long sales cycle | Meta (nurture) + Google (BOFU) |
| E-commerce with strong visuals | Both: Meta discovery, Google capture |
| Niche interest-based targeting | Meta |

**Rule:** Google captures demand. Meta creates demand. Best ROI = both, funnel-aligned.
