# Email Templates

## Newsletter Template

```html
Subject: [Value-focused headline]
Preview: [Expand on subject, 40-90 chars]

Hi [First Name],

[Opening hook - timely, relevant]

## This Week's Highlights

### [Article 1 Title]
[2-3 sentence summary]
[Read More →]

### [Article 2 Title]
[2-3 sentence summary]
[Read More →]

## Quick Tip
[Actionable advice in 2-3 sentences]

## What's Coming
[Tease next content]

[Signature]

P.S. [Soft CTA or bonus tip]
```

## Promotional Email

```html
Subject: [Benefit + Urgency if applicable]
Preview: [Details on offer]

Hi [First Name],

[Pain point or desire statement]

That's why we're excited to share [offer].

**What You Get:**
✓ [Benefit 1]
✓ [Benefit 2]
✓ [Benefit 3]

[CTA Button: Clear action verb]

[Social proof - testimonial or stat]

[Secondary CTA or details]

[Signature]

P.S. [Urgency or bonus reminder]
```

## Welcome Email

```html
Subject: Welcome to [Brand]! Here's what's next
Preview: Your first steps + a special gift

Hi [First Name],

Welcome! I'm thrilled you're here.

Here's what to expect:
1. [Weekly newsletter]
2. [Resource type]
3. [Community access]

**Your First Step:**
[Primary CTA - most valuable action]

**Questions?**
Just reply to this email - I read every response.

[Signature]

P.S. [Link to most popular resource]
```

## Re-engagement Email

```html
Subject: We miss you, [First Name]
Preview: Come back + special offer

Hi [First Name],

It's been a while since we've seen you.

We've added some exciting updates:
• [New feature/content 1]
• [New feature/content 2]

[CTA: Come back and explore]

Not interested anymore? [Unsubscribe link]

[Signature]
```

## Abandoned Cart Email

```html
Subject: Did you forget something?

[Complete Your Order]

**Need help?**
Reply to this email or [contact support].

```
