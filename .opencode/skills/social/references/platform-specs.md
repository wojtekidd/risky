# Platform Specifications

## Content Limits & Optimal Sizes

| Platform | Text Limit | Image Size | Video Length | Best Times |
|----------|------------|------------|--------------|------------|
| LinkedIn | 3000 chars | 1200x627 | 10 min | Tue-Thu 8-10am |
| Twitter/X | 280 chars | 1600x900 | 2:20 | Tue-Fri 9am-12pm |
| Instagram | 2200 chars | 1080x1080 | 90 sec (Reels) | Mon-Fri 11am-2pm |
| Facebook | 63K chars | 1200x630 | 240 min | Wed-Fri 1-4pm |
| TikTok | 4000 chars | 1080x1920 | 10 min | Tue-Thu 7-9pm |

## Platform-Specific Features

### LinkedIn
- Document carousels (PDF, 10 slides max)
- LinkedIn Live
- Newsletters
- Polls (2 weeks max)
- Articles (long-form)

### Twitter/X
- Threads (unlimited tweets)
- Spaces (audio rooms)
- Communities
- Polls (7 days max)
- Circle (private sharing)

### Instagram
- Stories (24hr, 15 sec clips)
- Reels (up to 90 sec)
- Carousels (10 images)
- Guides (curated collections)
- Broadcast channels

### Facebook
- Groups
- Events
- Live video
- Stories
- Reels

### TikTok
- Duets
- Stitches
- Effects/filters
- Live streams
- Photo mode
