# Marketing KPIs Framework

## Acquisition Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Benchmark |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| CAC | Total Marketing Cost / New Customers | Varies by industry |
| CPL | Total Marketing Cost / Leads | $5-50 B2C, $50-500 B2B |
| CPC | Ad Spend / Clicks | $0.50-5.00 |
| CPM | (Ad Spend / Impressions) × 1000 | $5-30 |
| Traffic | Sessions, Users | Growth trend |

## Engagement Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Benchmark |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| CTR | Clicks / Impressions × 100 | 1-3% ads, 2-5% email |
| Bounce Rate | Single Page Visits / Total Visits | 40-60% |
| Time on Site | Total Time / Sessions | 2-3 minutes |
| Pages/Session | Pageviews / Sessions | 2-4 pages |
| Engagement Rate | Engaged Sessions / Total Sessions | 50-70% |

## Conversion Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Benchmark |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| CVR | Conversions / Visitors × 100 | 2-5% |
| Lead-to-Customer | Customers / Leads × 100 | 10-20% |
| ROAS | Revenue / Ad Spend | 4:1+ profitable |
| AOV | Revenue / Orders | Industry specific |
| Cart Abandonment | Abandoned / Initiated × 100 | 70% average |

## Retention Metrics

| Metric | Formula | Benchmark |
|--------|---------|-----------|
| LTV | Avg Order × Purchase Freq × Lifespan | 3x CAC minimum |
| Churn Rate | Lost Customers / Total × 100 | <5% monthly |
| Retention Rate | Retained / Starting × 100 | >95% monthly |
| NPS | Promoters - Detractors | >50 excellent |
| Repeat Purchase | Returning / Total Customers | 20-40% |

## Channel-Specific KPIs

### Email
- Open Rate: 15-25%
- Click Rate: 2-5%
- Unsubscribe: <0.5%

### Social
- Engagement Rate: 1-5%
- Share Rate: 0.5-1%
- Follower Growth: 2-5%/month

### SEO
- Organic Traffic Growth
- Keyword Rankings
- Backlink Growth

### Paid
- Quality Score: 7+
- Impression Share
- ROAS/CPA
