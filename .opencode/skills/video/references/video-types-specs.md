# Video Types & Platform Specifications

## Platform Specs

| Platform | Aspect Ratio | Resolution | Max Length | Ideal Length |
|----------|--------------|------------|------------|--------------|
| YouTube | 16:9 | 1920x1080 | 12 hours | 8-15 min |
| YouTube Shorts | 9:16 | 1080x1920 | 60 sec | 30-60 sec |
| TikTok | 9:16 | 1080x1920 | 10 min | 15-60 sec |
| Instagram Reels | 9:16 | 1080x1920 | 90 sec | 15-30 sec |
| Instagram Feed | 1:1/4:5 | 1080x1080 | 60 sec | 30-60 sec |
| LinkedIn | 16:9/1:1 | 1920x1080 | 10 min | 1-2 min |
| Facebook | 16:9/1:1 | 1920x1080 | 240 min | 1-3 min |

## Video Types

### Educational/Tutorial
- Teach skills or concepts
- Step-by-step format
- 5-15 min (YouTube), 30-60 sec (short-form)

### Product Demo
- Showcase features/benefits
- Use cases in action
- 2-5 min (long), 15-30 sec (short)

### Testimonial
- Customer success stories
- Authentic, relatable
- 1-3 min

### Behind-the-Scenes
- Build connection
- Show process/team
- Casual, authentic

### Explainer
- Complex topics simplified
- Animation/graphics
- 60-90 sec

### Short-form/Viral
- Entertainment-first
- Trend-based
- 15-60 sec

## Technical Requirements

### Audio
- Sample rate: 48kHz
- Format: AAC/MP3
- Normalize to -14 LUFS

### Export Settings
- Codec: H.264
- Bitrate: 10-20 Mbps
- Frame rate: 24/30/60 fps
