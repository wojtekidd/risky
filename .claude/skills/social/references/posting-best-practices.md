# Social Media Posting Best Practices

## LinkedIn
- Professional tone, insights-driven
- Use line breaks for readability
- Include 3-5 hashtags at end
- Tag relevant connections
- Add document carousels for engagement
- Post native video over links
- Engage in comments for 1 hour post-publish

## Twitter/X
- Conversational, punchy style
- Use threads for longer content
- Include 1-2 hashtags max
- Reply to grow visibility
- Retweet with commentary
- Use visuals to boost engagement
- Time-sensitive content works well

## Instagram
- Visual-first, high-quality images
- Carousels outperform single posts
- Use Stories for daily engagement
- Reels for reach/discovery
- 20-30 hashtags in first comment
- Strong CTA in bio link
- User-generated content performs well

## Facebook
- Community-focused content
- Native video preferred
- Ask questions to drive engagement
- Groups for community building
- Events for local/live activities
- Share stories and behind-scenes

## TikTok
- Authentic, not polished
- Hook in first 3 seconds
- Use trending sounds
- Participate in challenges
- Respond with video comments
- Post 1-3x per day for growth

## Universal Best Practices

1. **Consistency > Frequency**
   - Better to post 3x/week consistently than 7x one week, 0 the next

2. **Engage Before/After Posting**
   - 15-30 min engagement before and after posting

3. **Use Platform-Native Features**
   - Algorithms favor native content

4. **Respond to Comments Quickly**
   - Within 1 hour ideally

5. **Test and Iterate**
   - Track what works for YOUR audience
